### Salvage

Salvage items are generally obtained by a [salvager](https://elite-dangerous.fandom.com/wiki/Salvager "Salvager") or [pirate](https://elite-dangerous.fandom.com/wiki/Pirate "Pirate") from sites containing preexisting wreckage, abandoned structures, or alien structures. Salvage is usually marked [stolen](https://elite-dangerous.fandom.com/wiki/Stolen_Goods "Stolen Goods") or [illegal](https://elite-dangerous.fandom.com/wiki/Illegal_Goods "Illegal Goods") in most systems. Certain Salvage items, such as Escape Pods and Black Boxes, can be given to the Search & Rescue Contact available at most Stations.

### Slavery

Slaves are beings forced into labor and servitude, usually against their will and with no compensation or legal protections. Conventional slavery is illegal in most systems other than Anarchies, but [Imperial Slavery](https://elite-dangerous.fandom.com/wiki/Imperial_Slavery "Imperial Slavery"), a regulated form of indentured servitude, is legal in the [Empire](https://elite-dangerous.fandom.com/wiki/Empire "Empire") and heavily integrated into its culture.
